Headings and paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> - <h6>
    • <h1>Heading 1</h1>
    <h2>Sub heading 2</h2>
    <h3>Sub heading 2</h3>
  • Paragraph Tags: <p>
    • <p>This is my first paragraph</p>
    <p>This is my second paragraph</>
  • Sections: <div> and <space>
    • <div style="background: skyblue;">
    This is a div </div>
